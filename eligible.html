Eligibility for Participation

Any or every person who knows any programming language and is familiar with general concepts like looping & arrays, can participate in the event. Participants can join from any part of the world. Those who are at the initial stage of learning are allowed to participate in teams (max. 2 individuals/team). It is compulsory for all participants to abide to the Honor of Code for the contest.
